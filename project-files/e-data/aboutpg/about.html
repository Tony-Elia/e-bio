This is a project for registration of MA degree in education
This project or research aims to:

-Identify the appropriate skills for the design and dissemination of the electronic content that should be available to learn biology in secondary schools.

-Provide a proposed therapeutic program for the use of electronic marking instead of Tablets in assessments.

-Designing Electronic program based on the connectivism theory by using of tablet to develop academic achievement of biology for secondary school students

-Help students of secondary school enriching thier information about Biology, testing thier level of study and facilating studying biology.
